Our Mission & Values

Restoring ecosystems, creating green spaces, and inspiring environmental stewardship through professional tree planting and landscaping.

Why We Exist

Our planet's health depends on thriving ecosystems and green infrastructure. We believe that every community deserves beautiful, sustainable landscapes that support both nature and people. That's why My Pet Tree exists: to plant trees, restore ecosystems, and empower our communities to build a greener, more resilient future together.

Our Core Values

Community Impact

We work to strengthen local ecosystems and create shared green spaces that benefit everyone.

Expertise

Our certified arborists and landscape professionals bring proven knowledge and best practices to every project.

Innovation

We adopt cutting-edge techniques in tree care, habitat restoration, and sustainable landscaping practices.

Our Story

My Pet Tree was founded on the belief that environmental restoration starts with a single seed. What began as a passion project to plant native trees in our community has grown into a professional landscaping and conservation company.

Today, we've planted over 50,000 trees and restored 150+ acres of landscape across our region. We've built our reputation on quality workmanship, ecological expertise, and genuine commitment to environmental stewardship.

Our vision is to be the go-to partner for communities and individuals who want to actively participate in creating a healthier, more sustainable future.
