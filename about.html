Our Story

From a Single Seed to 50,000 Trees

My Pet Tree was founded in 2015 on the belief that environmental restoration starts with a single seed. What began as a passion project to plant native trees in our community has grown into a professional landscaping and conservation company serving hundreds of clients.

Today, we've planted over 50,000 trees and restored 150+ acres of landscape across our region. We've built our reputation on quality workmanship, ecological expertise, and genuine commitment to environmental stewardship.

Our Vision: To be the go-to partner for communities and individuals who want to actively participate in creating a healthier, more sustainable future.
