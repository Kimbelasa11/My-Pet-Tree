Quick answers to common questions about our services.

What species of trees do you plant?

We work with local experts to select native species that thrive in your region's climate and soil. Popular choices include maples, oaks, birches, and fruit trees.

How long do trees typically live?

Most trees we plant have lifespans of 50-150+ years, depending on species and care. Proper watering, pruning, and stewardship significantly extend their lives.
